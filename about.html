Heritage Hunt turns cultural exploration into a simple, joyful experience. Discover hidden heritage sites around you and help protect them for the future.

auto_stories Why Heritage Hunt?

Many important cultural sites are overlooked, undocumented, or slowly fading from public memory. Heritage Hunt encourages people to physically explore, learn about, and acknowledge these places turning everyday citizens into guardians of culture.

There’s no account, no pressure just curiosity, exploration, and respect for the stories that shaped our world.

public UN Sustainable Development Goal 11.4

Protecting Cultural Heritage

SDG 11.4 focuses on safeguarding the world’s cultural and natural heritage. Every site you discover through Heritage Hunt contributes to awareness, respect, and long-term preservation.
