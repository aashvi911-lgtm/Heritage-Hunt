auto_stories About NMIMS

What started with baby steps in 1981, NMIMS has today emerged as a globally reputed university. Always socially conscious, Shri Vile Parle Kelavani Mandal (SVKM) made the decision to cater to the rising demand for management institutes in the country. This led to the birth of Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies (NMIMS). It began humbly, offering a two-year full-time master’s programme in management studies with four full-time faculty members, 3,162 books, and an intake of 40 students, operating from Bhaidas Hall, Vile Parle (West), Mumbai.
