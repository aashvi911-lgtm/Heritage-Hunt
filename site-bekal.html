Kerala, India

Bekal Fort

auto_stories The Story

Bekal Fort is the largest fort in Kerala, built along the Arabian Sea coastline. Unlike residential forts, it was designed mainly for military defense and surveillance, featuring wide observation points and thick laterite walls overlooking the sea.

stars Why It’s Special

Bekal Fort’s open design and strategic coastal placement show how historical architecture adapted to geography and security needs. Its structure prioritizes visibility, airflow, and defense rather than religious or royal symbolism.
